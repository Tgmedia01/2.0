Project
Overview

The Hair Room is a luxury hair salon based in Earlsdon — one of Coventry's most established residential areas. A full-service salon offering premium hair services in a space built around calm, quality and a personalised client experience.

The project required a website that could stand alongside premium salons competing for the same clientele — plus a comprehensive local SEO strategy to establish visibility across Coventry and the surrounding area.

02
Challenge

The
Challenge

"A premium salon deserves a premium first impression. The website was the first place that impression was being lost."

Hair and beauty is one of the most competitive local search markets in any UK city. Without structured SEO, a salon of genuine quality can be effectively invisible to the clients who would most value it — and converting those clients to a booking becomes someone else's advantage.

Approach

Booking
First.

01

Premium Before Anything

The design was built to communicate the quality of the physical space before a prospective client visits. Warm, editorial, calm — the digital environment mirrors the atmosphere the salon delivers in person.

02

Conversion Architecture

Every page structured to move the visitor from discovery to confidence to action. The booking CTA is persistent — always one step away without being intrusive. No dead ends, no ambiguity about what to do next.

03

Local SEO From the Ground Up

Rather than treating SEO as an afterthought, we built the page architecture and content structure around local search intent from day one. Schema markup, keyword-targeted service pages and Open Graph across every page type.

Design System

Calm.
Considered.

The visual language built around the two things a premium salon client is looking for before they commit — calm and quality. Nothing included that didn't serve that goal.

Typography

Aa

Refined serif for brand headings. Clean sans for service listings and body copy. Type communicates luxury without formality — nothing shouts in a premium environment.

Colour

Warm neutrals and near-whites — referencing the interior of a well-designed salon space. Deep contrast accents for CTAs and navigation only.

Photography

Salon interior photography at full width — letting the physical space speak for itself. Team photography with consistent styling. Before/after presented with clinical precision, not social media informality.

Layout

Generous white space throughout — a luxury signal in itself. Service sections structured and scannable. The layout guides from introduction to services to booking without effort or confusion.

Key Feature

Local SEO
Architecture

For an independent salon in a competitive local market, structured search visibility is a commercial asset. We built it in from day one rather than retrofitting it afterwards.

Local Business schema — name, address, phone, opening hours

Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags across all pages

Yoast SEO — meta titles, descriptions, canonical tags

Service-specific page architecture targeting Coventry search terms

Image optimisation and structured alt text for search indexing

WordPress / Divi CMS — full team control post-launch
