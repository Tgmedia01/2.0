This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how TG Media uses them on this website (tgmedia.uk), and what your options are. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, remember your preferences, and — in some cases — track user behaviour for analytics or advertising purposes.

Cookies are categorised as either strictly necessary (required for the site to function), functional (improve your experience), analytics (track usage), or marketing (advertising). Each category requires a different level of consent under UK GDPR and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

Cookies We Use

This website currently uses only the following minimal cookies and technical processes:

Source Type Purpose Consent Required

Cloudflare / jsDelivr CDN Functional Loads JavaScript libraries (GSAP, Tailwind, Lucide) used for site functionality and animations. No — technically necessary for functionality

We do not currently use:

  • Google Analytics or any other analytics platform
  • Facebook Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Any cookie that tracks your behaviour across other websites

Because we only use technically necessary cookies, we are not currently required to display a cookie consent banner. If this changes — for example if we add analytics — we will update this policy and add a consent mechanism accordingly.
