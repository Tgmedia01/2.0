Overview

Project
Overview

Purple Granite is one of the UK's most established stone worktop specialists — operating showrooms in Kidderminster and Worcester, fabricating and installing entirely in-house. A direct operation that gives them a significant quality and speed advantage over the market.

The project required a complete digital overhaul to match the premium positioning of the product range and better serve the modern home buyer who researches entirely online before committing.

02
Challenge

The
Challenge

"The gap between product catalogue and customer confidence was costing enquiries. We needed to close that gap with technology."

Stone worktops are a considered purchase. Customers can't visualise how a material will look in their kitchen from a flat product photo. A significant proportion were dropping off before enquiry — unable to bridge that gap.

Approach

UX
First.

01

Browse, Visualise, Enquire

The architecture was restructured around three core user intentions. Every design decision was made in service of moving the user efficiently through those three stages without friction or doubt.

02

Product Led

Stone photography at maximum scale. Stone textures deserve space. Product grids are generous — fewer products per row, each given room to communicate material quality. White space is as much a design element as the photography.

03

Conversion Architecture

Every page has a single, unambiguous next step. Sample request, showroom booking or quote enquiry — always one step away, never buried. No dead ends in the buying journey.

Key Feature — Custom Build

The Worktop
Visualiser

A bespoke interactive tool that removes the core friction in the buying journey — letting customers see exactly how a material looks in their kitchen before committing.

Material Library

Full product range — granite, quartz, marble and porcelain — rendered with accurate colour and vein mapping inside a real kitchen environment.

Real-time Switching

Instant material application — no reload, no delay. Customers compare options side by side in a single session without friction or waiting.

Kitchen Environments

Multiple scene options covering different cabinet colours and styles — customers can match to their specific kitchen context, not just a generic render.

Direct to Enquiry

From the visualiser, customers move directly to sample request, showroom booking or quote — all within one continuous, uninterrupted journey.

Development

Built for
Performance.

Built on Shopify with a fully custom theme. The visualiser is a bespoke JavaScript application integrated into product pages — canvas-based rendering applies material maps to the kitchen environment in real time.

High-resolution material textures with optimised asset loading maintain speed across all devices including mobile. Tested extensively across viewport sizes, connection speeds and device types before launch.

Scalable Shopify platform supporting the full product range and future growth
