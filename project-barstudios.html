Overview

Project
Overview

Bar Studios is a social-first content production studio working with some of the world's most recognisable brands — Red Bull, major sports organisations and global consumer labels. Their output spans documentary, short-form, campaign production and live event coverage.

The project required a platform that immediately communicates production quality to prospective brand and agency clients evaluating content partners at the highest level.

02
Challenge

The
Challenge

"When your clients include Red Bull, your website needs to feel like it belongs in the same room. It wasn't. We changed that."

Content production studios are judged on the quality of their output. A brand director evaluating production partners will spend less than thirty seconds on a portfolio site before forming a view. Bar Studios' existing presence was underselling the studio to the exact audience they most needed to impress.

Approach

Work
First.

01

The Work Leads

One organising principle. Every structural and visual decision made in service of getting the user to Bar Studios' best content as quickly as possible, in an environment that lets it land at the scale it deserves.

02

Editorial Grid

A magazine-style project archive — asymmetric, deliberately varied, reflecting the variety of production output. Larger placements reserved for flagship campaigns. Nothing uniform, nothing generic.

03

Fast to Contact

New business enquiries one step from any point on the site. Friction between interest and contact has been removed entirely. The studio should never lose a lead to a missing button.

Design System

Identity &
Visual Language

Monochrome primary. Editorial. Bold. The visual language draws from fashion publishing — a design system built to complement rather than compete with the visual content it presents.

Typography

Extended sans-serif. Wide tracking, high contrast. Headline type aggressive in scale and confident in weight — references editorial publishing, not digital convention.

Colour

Monochrome by design. Colour enters exclusively through project photography. The neutral backdrop gives content maximum impact.

Grid

Asymmetric editorial grid for the project archive. Deliberately non-uniform — reflects the variety and dynamism of Bar Studios' output. Projects at different scales within the grid.

Motion

Video-first homepage. Featured projects autoplay on loop — muted, cropped for impact. Hover states reveal project title and category with clean opacity transitions. Nothing gratuitous.
