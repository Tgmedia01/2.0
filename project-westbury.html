Westbury Music required a digital presence that reflected its position within the global music licensing industry — balancing creative culture with commercial credibility.

We collaborated closely with Anti-Ordinary, who led brand development, while we engineered the digital system — ensuring the visual language translated seamlessly into a scalable web platform. The result is a platform built on structured thinking, not decoration.

The Approach

The identity developed by Anti-Ordinary carried clarity and restraint. Our role was to preserve that integrity while adapting it for digital interaction. Typography, grid structure and motion were carefully implemented to maintain consistency across every device and breakpoint.
