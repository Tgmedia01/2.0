A digital platform built for the global music licensing industry.

Westbury Music required a digital presence that reflected its position within the global music licensing industry — balancing creative culture with commercial credibility.

We collaborated closely with Anti-Ordinary, who led brand development, while we engineered the digital system — ensuring the visual language translated seamlessly into a scalable web platform.

The result is a platform that operates at the intersection of creative credibility and commercial precision.

02 — THE APPROACH

Brand Translation

The identity developed by Anti-Ordinary carried clarity and restraint. Our role was to preserve that integrity while adapting it for digital interaction.

Typography, grid structure and motion were carefully implemented to maintain consistency across all devices and contexts.

Digital Architecture

We designed and developed a modular website framework allowing Westbury Music to:

  • →Showcase licensed music in film & television
  • →Present artists with clarity and structure
  • →Maintain performance across global audiences
