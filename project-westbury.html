Overview

Westbury Music required a digital presence that reflected its position within the global music licensing industry — balancing creative culture with commercial credibility.

We collaborated closely with Anti-Ordinary, who led brand development, while we engineered the digital system — ensuring the visual language translated seamlessly into a scalable web platform.

This project demonstrates the importance of structured collaboration — where creative direction and technical execution operate in alignment, without compromise on either side.
