01
Overview

Project
Overview

Alpine MI is a London-based commercial fit-out company specialising in high-end retail and commercial interiors. They transform empty spaces into immersive brand environments — delivering full fit-outs for clients who demand both precision and aesthetics.

The project required a series of professionally designed case study documents — pitch-ready PDF collateral that Alpine MI could use to present completed projects, demonstrate capability, and win new business in a competitive market.

02
Challenge

The
Challenge

"The quality of the work was exceptional. The problem was that nothing they were handing to clients reflected that."

Alpine MI were operating at a high level — delivering complex, premium fit-outs for brands and businesses across London — but their client-facing materials didn't match. Generic documents and informal presentations were underselling the capability of the company to exactly the clients who could afford to care.

Approach

Print.
Done
Properly.

01

Document Architecture

Each case study was structured to tell the project story clearly and sequentially — from the client brief and context through to the completed environment. Structure before style. The reader always knows where they are and what they're looking at.

02

Photography-Led Design

Alpine MI's completed spaces are visually striking — the document design was built to let the photography lead. Large-format images, full-bleed spreads and minimal chrome around them. The work does the talking.

03

Pitch-Ready Format

Designed as both a printed leave-behind and a high-resolution digital PDF. Every element considered for how it reads in a boardroom, on screen and on paper — consistent quality across all delivery formats.

Document Design

Inside the
Documents

Each spread designed to a print specification — 3mm bleed, CMYK colour, high-resolution imagery, and a typographic system that holds up at A4 and A3.

Design System

Type &
System

A document design system that works across multiple case studies — consistent grid, type hierarchy and colour application — so new documents can be produced quickly to the same standard.

Typography

Aa

Two-weight system — bold black display type for headings and section titles, regular weight for body copy. Clean, authoritative, print-optimised. Holds at small sizes without losing legibility.

Colour

Monochrome primary — black, white and mid-greys. The photography provides all the colour. Brand accent used sparingly for section labels and rule lines only.

Layout

A5 landscape format across all documents — optimal for both printed presentation and digital viewing. Consistent margins, a structured column grid, and full-bleed photography sections that contrast with the white editorial pages.
